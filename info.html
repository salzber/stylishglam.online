Return & Exchange Policy

We want you to be absolutely captivated by your purchase. If a piece isn't a perfect match, we offer a seamless and elegant return experience.

  • Return requests must be initiated within 7 days of receiving your delivery.
  • Garments must remain unworn, unwashed, and in their pristine original condition with all Stylishglam tags attached.
  • Reimbursements will be issued to the original form of payment within 5-7 business days following our thorough quality inspection.
  • Please note that items acquired during exclusive clearance or final sale events are non-refundable.
